初高中衔接：

🌈1.时态

  • 时态分为时间（现在、过去、将来、过去将来）与特点（一般、完成、进行、完成进行）。所以时态的组合就是时间+特点

  • 时间 & 特点 一般 完成 进行 完成进行
    现在 一般现在时：
    do / does    		 现在完成时：
    have / has done    		 现在进行时：
    be doing    		 现在完成进行时：
    have / has been doing
    过去 一般过去时：
    did    		 过去完成时：
    had done    		 过去进行时：
    be doing    		 过去完成进行时：
    had been doing
    将来 一般将来时：
    be going to do    		 将来完成时：
    will have done    		 过去进行时：
    will be done    		 过去完成进行时：
    will have been doing
    过去将来 一般过去将来时：
    was / were going to do    		 过去将来完成时：
    would have done    		 过去将来进行时：
    would be doing    		 过去将来完成进行时：
    would have been doing

句式结构（句法）

一、句子成分

1.主语（Subject）

​ 1.1定义：是句子叙述的主体（中心人物or事物，动作的==发起者==），表明这句话的“主角”名词、代词、数词、名词化形容词、不定式短语、动名词、从句均可以担任主语

可作主语 例句
名词 The apple is red. / The banana is yellow.
代词 Nobody can help you but yourself.
不定式短语 To becomes a nurse is my wish
从句 That he forget to tell me the time for the meeting caused lots of trouble
数词 Four and five makes nine.（做整体用三单）
动名词 Running is good for health
名词化形容词 The sick and the old need your help（sick & old是形容词名词化，表示一类人）

2.谓语（Predicate）

​ 2.1定义：表示主语的动作或状态，由动词担任，分为==简单谓语==和==复合谓语==

简单谓语：由动词或动词词组充当 复合谓语：“情态动词或助动词 + 动词” / “系动词 + 表语”
He often reads books. / The plane takes off at 8. He can speak English（情态动词 + 动词）.
The work will be finished（助动词 + 动词）
The book is interesting（系动词 + 表语）

3.宾语（Object）

​ 3.1定义：表示及物动词和及物动词短语的对象或内容（动作的承受者） / 用于介词后面构成介宾短语。名词、代词、数词、不定时、名词化的形容词、动名词或从句均可以担任宾语

可作宾语 例句
名词： We love our motherland.
代词： Where did you buy that?
数词： If you put 5 and 7 together, you will get 12.
不定式： I wish to call on you.
名词化形容词： We should help the poor.
动名词短语： I would advise waiting until tomorrow.
从句： Do you understand what I mean?

4.定语（Attribute）

​ 4.1定义：“修饰”或“限定”“名词”或“代词”的成分叫定语（==形容词==多做定语）。限定 / 修饰：将大范围的缩小至某个或某个整体。

可作定语 例句
形容词： It’s a fine day today.
副词： The building around were badly damaged
介词短语： The books on the top shelf were just bought.
代词： Open your mouth and put out your tongue.
数词： She cut the cake into two pieces.
名词： This is stone table.
动名词： Put the child in the sleeping bag
不定式短语： Let’s try another way to do this.
现在分词： This is moving film.
过去分词： Please bring me some boiled water

5.状语（Adverbial）

​ 5.1定义：

状语修饰动词、形容词、副词或全句等。他可以表示时间、地点、方式、比较、成都、原因、目的、结果、条件和让 步等。

可作状语 例句
副词： Tom speaks English very fast.
不定时短语： We work to live not live to work.
分词短语： Arriving there call me up.
介词短语： The meeting is going to be held in Room321 tomorrow morning.
形容词短语： He returned home, safe and sound.
从句： We’ll go where the people need us.
独立主格结构： Time permitting, we’ll take a break.

6.补语（Complement）

​ 6.1定义：宾语补足语是指位于==宾语==之后，对==宾语==进行补充说明的成分。往里面（宾语）填充内容，使他变得更加饱满

可作补语 例句
形容词： I found the book very interesting.
分词： Do you hear Tom singing.
不定式短语： She asked me to lend her a hand.
名词： We must keep it a secret.
介词短语： Please make yourself at home.
副词： Please keep the dog out

7.表语（Predicative）

​ 7.1定义：说明主语特征、类属、状态或身份，名词、代词、形容词、数词、副词、介词短语、不定时、动名词、分词或从句均可以担任表语（一般情况下，系动词后均是表语）

可作表语 例句
名词： Pollution from cars has become a major problem.
代词： Seventy-four! You don’t look it.
数词： Angela was the first to leave.
形容词： Please feel free to say what you really think.
动名词： Wasting time is killing yourself.
不定式： My wish is to become an artist.
现在分词： Time is pressing.
副词： The five was still on when we arrived.
介词短语： Come to see me tomorrow when I am at work.
从句： This is where we disagree with each other.

8.同位语（Apposition）

​ 8.1定义：对句子中某一名词（词组）作进一步解释说明，与前面的名词（词组）在语法上处于同等地位。同位语常常置于被说明的词后。==【！与补语不同，补语属于补充（填充内容），同位语属于并列关系的解释与说明】==

可作同位语 例句
名词： We have two foreign teachers, a Canadian and an American.
代词： They each can get chance to travel by air
数词： We two will go to see you.
从句： The news that he will come is true.

​ 8.2🔖备注：对于抽象名词的解释也是同位语的一种！;下划线内容为名词（被解释对象）；橙色字体为同位语（解释名词）

二、句子结构

1.简单句：

​ 1.1主 + 谓（S + VI）——VI是不及物动词

​ 1.1.1谓语动词均为==不及物动词==，及不能直接加宾语~

​ ✔He laughed. / The moon rose.

​ 1.2主 + 谓 + 宾（S + VT + O）——VT是及物动词

​ 1.2.1谓语动词均为==及物动词（短语）==，直接加宾语

​ ✔I love you. / He enjoys reading.

​ 1.3主 + 谓 + 间宾 + 直宾（S + V + IO + DO）——V表示告诉 or 给

​ 1.3.1此类举行的谓语动词通常是表示==“给、告诉”一类的动词==，例如：give（sb. sth.）, offer（sb. sth）, tell.

​ ✔I will give you my heart.

​ ✔She bought herself a dress.

​ 1.4主 + 谓 + 宾 + 宾补（S + V + O + C）——V为感官动词、使役动词

​ 1.4.1此类举行谓语通常为==感官动词==（look, sound, taste, feel）或==使役动词==（make, let, get）

​ ✔You make me happy,

​ ✔I have my hair cut every month.

​ 1.5主 + 系 + 表（S + LV + P）

​ 1.5.1系表结构表示主语的状态、特征或性质！

​ ✔I saw her playing the piano in the hall.

​ ✔The dinner smells good.

​ 🔖备注：“be + done”既可以表示被动语态，也可以表示系表结构。但被动语态不接宾语！可以以此分辨主系表结构 or 被动

2.并列句

2.1并列关系：

​ and（和）, both…and…（和）, not only…but(also)…（不但…而且）等

​ and 的三层含义，1.“和”，表并列；2.“而且”，表递进；3.“然后”，表动作先后

​ ✔I can speak English and Math!（表示并列关系，…和…）

​ ✔I did my homework and did it really terrible（表示递进关系，做了，==而且==很不好）

​ ✔I began to play first, and do homework at last（表示动作的先后）

​ ✔Both Tom and Bob are going to do homework.

​ ✔I not only heard it, but also saw it（强调后面，不但，而且）

2.2转折关系

​ but（但是）, yet（可是）, however（可是），while（而，另一方面）等

​ ✔I hate do Math homework, but I don’t really hate this subject.

​ ✔He worked hard, yet he failed.

​ ✔It’s raining hard, however, we must go on.

​ ✔He wants to be a doctor, while his mother wants he became a writer.

2.3因果关系

​ for（因为）, so（所以）等

​ ✔He never do homework, for he has graduated for a long time.

​ ✔He do the chores once a week, so his room is really untidy.

2.4选择关系

​ or, neither…nor…（既不…也不…）, either…or…（要么…要么…）等

​ or 的两层含义，1.“或者”，表选择；2.“否则”，表条件

​ ✔You can choose hamburger or bread.

​ ✔You must tell the truth or you will be punished.

​ ✔He neither washes the car, nor polishes it.

​ ✔I either do homework, or study English.

2.5其他关系

​ not…but（不是…而是…）, rather than（而不是；与其…宁愿…）, when（那是=at that time）等

​ ✔It was not a good choose but the best choose.【这个不是一个好的选择，而是最好的选择】

​ ✔Rather than ride on a crowded bus, he always prefers to ride a bicycle.【他宁愿骑自行车，也不愿坐拥挤的公共汽车。】

2.6特殊

​ 2.6.1when / while, when做并列连词时，表示“这时、那时”，相当于and at this / that time；while做并列连词，表示“而、却”，表示对比（见上“转折关系”最后一条）
